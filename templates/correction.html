Choosing methods

Microbiome-oriented methods are useful when sparsity, compositionality, and zero inflation are central to the analysis. General-purpose methods remain useful baselines, especially after appropriate transformation. Severe batch-target confounding can make any correction difficult to interpret, so the upload pre-check warns users without blocking the workflow.
